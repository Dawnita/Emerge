Understanding Substance Abuse

Addiction is a chronic disease that affects nearly 20 million American Men. Unfortunately, there’s a terrible stigma that surrounds addiction, which makes it difficult for some to seek out mens recovery program on their own.

Addiction Treatment for Men

If a loved one is suffering from addiction, there is hope. While it might seem like you have nowhere to turn, Emerge for men provides a supportive environment where you can connect with like-minded individuals and work towards recovery.

Typically speaking, men are more likely to start using drugs and therefore, fall into addiction. Men are also more likely to start using drugs due to peer pressure, which shows us the strength of group influence both in addiction and in men’s recovery programs.
